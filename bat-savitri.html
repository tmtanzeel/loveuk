Bat Savitri

Description

  • This festival is celebrated on the Krishna amavasya (last day of the dark half of the month) of Jyestha.
  • Married women worship Savitri and the Bat or banyan tree (Ficus benghalensis) and pray for the well being of their spouses.
  • Women observe fast in honour of Savitri and Satyavan and remember how Savitri through her intense devotion saved her husband from the claws of death.
