Khatarua

Description

The victory of the king of Kumaon is also said to be one of the reasons for the celebration of Khatarua.
  • Khatarua is essentially the special festival of pastoral- agricultural society
    and celebrated on the first day of the month of Ashwin in mid September,
    and signifies the beginning of the autumn.
  • On this day people light bonfires, around which children dance, holding aloft colourful flags.
  • People take special care of their animals and feed them fresh grass.
  • Cucumbers are offered to the fire of Khatarua, which is said to destroy all evil influences.
