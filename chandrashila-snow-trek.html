Chandrashila Snow Trek

Description

  • Region: Garhwal Himalayas of Uttarakhand
  • Altitude : 4090 mts/13415 ft
  • Duration : 5 Days
  • Season : Mid November to Mid March
  • Grade : Easy

Trekking Routes for Chandrashila Snow Trekking

Delhi - Haridwar - Ukhimath - Chopta - Tungnath - Chandrashila - Chopta - Haridwar - Delhi

Highlights of the trip

  • Chopta is one of the most beautiful and picturesque spots in the whole Garhwal.
  • The breathtaking view of the Himalayas is treat for the eyes as well as soul.
  • Chopta is situated on the Gopeshwar - Ukhimath road at a distance of about 40 Kms from Gopeshwar Chopta is 2900 mts high.
  • Chandrashila Trek is one of the popular treks that can be undertaken round the year.
  • Chandrashila summit is a rock face above the temple of Tungnath.
  • The Chandrashila summit affords the most beautiful view of the Nanda Devi, Trishul, Kedar Peak and Chaukhamba peaks.

Detailed trek

Day 01 : Arrive Delhi On arrival at Delhi airport meet our representative and transfer to pre booked hotel. Overnight stay.

Day 02 : Delhi - Haridwar - Ukhimath (200 kms/7-8 hrs) In the morning board an AC train for Haridwar at 06:55 hrs. Arrival at Haridwar by 11:25 hrs and then transfer to Ukhimath via Rishikesh, Devprayag (Confluence of Alaknanda & Bhagirathi, (the river is named "Ganges" after the Devprayag confluence). Arrival at Ukhimath by evening. Check into TRH. Dinner and overnight stay. Ukhimath (1370 mts/4500 ft) is on the far side of the Mandakini River Valley and is the winter seat of the Kedarnath shrine, as well as the winter home of the priest who presides over the Kedarnath Temple. After the Kedarnath temple closes for the winters, a movable image of Lord Kedarnath is brought in a special procession to Ukhimath, where it resides until the Kedarnath temple reopens in late April or early May, and is visited by tens of thousands every season. The temples of Usha and Anirudh, & Shiva and Parvati are worth a visit. On arrival at Delhi airport meet our representative and transfer to pre booked hotel. Overnight stay.

Day 03: Ukhimath - Chopta In the morning you can see snow clapped Himalayan ranges. After breakfast start drive to Chopta through dense forest of Rhodendron and lots of wild flowers and ferns. The drive will take an hrs to reach Chopta. Upon arrival at Chopta set out your tents. Enjoy the surrounding area by your own. In the evening explore the magnificent view of the Himalayas. Dinner & Overnight stay at Chopta.

Day 04 : Chopta - Tungnath (3680 ft/12070 ft)- Chandrashila (4090 mts/13415 ft) - Chopta After an early breakfast trek to Tungnath with packed lunch. Tungnath is situated at the height of 3680 mts from the sea level the highest in India. The trek is three kms upward. Enroute you will find lush green meadows and you can see the entire range of snow clapped Himalayas. Upon arrival at Tungnath Temple and visit the temple. After relax one further one kms trek to Chandrashila peak. Later trek back to Chopta for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 05 : Chopta - Haridwar - Delhi After an early breakfast drive to Haridwar. Upon arrival at Haridwar transfer to railway station to catch Dehradun Shatabdi express train for Delhi at 18:05 hrs. Arrival at Delhi by 22:45 hrs and then transfer to airport to catch onwards flight.

Tour & Service End.
