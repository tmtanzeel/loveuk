Trekkers Paradise

Trekking in Garhwal brings marvelous opportunities for adventurous lovers to find the ever inspiring peaks, beautiful valleys and meadows and to learn about the legends associated with all the important peaks and destinations that typically make Garhwal so renowned and special and thus makes the adventure holidays more meaningful and interesting.

Trekking in Garhwal gives many enjoyable and rejuvenating experiences plus gives a chance to meet the local simple living. The Garhwal trekking arranges for many adventurous touring activities including trekking tours, mountain biking tour, rafting tours and cultural tours in the fascinating and glorifying Himalayan region.
