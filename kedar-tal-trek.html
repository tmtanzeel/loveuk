Kedarnath Vasuki Tal Trek

Description

  • Region: Garhwal Himalayas
  • Altitude : 4090 mts/13415 ft
  • Duration : 08 Days
  • Season : Round the Year
  • Grade : Easy to Moderate

Trekking Routes for Kedarnath Vasuki Tal Trekking

Delhi - Hardwar - Rishikesh - Guptkashi - Gaurikund - Kedarnath - Gaurikund - Rudraprayag - Haridwar - Delhi

Highlights of the trip

  • Kedarnath is at an altitude of 3584 m.
  • A magnificent temple was built by the Pandavas at the base of Kedarnath peak's southeastern face.
  • The trek starts from Gaurikund, Where there is a hot water Sulphur Spring.
  • While approaching Kedarnath, we pass through stately forests and come across some beautiful waterfalls.
  • Kedarnath to Vasuki Tal is a continuous ascent, and a great trek.
  • Vasuki Tal Lake, about ½ kms in radius, with crystal clear waters

Detailed trek

Day 01 : Arrive- Delhi On arrival at Delhi airport meet our representative and transfer to pre booked hotel. Overnight stay.

Day 02 : Delhi - Hardwar - Rishikesh Morning transfer to the station time to catch train for Hardwar at 06:50 hrs. Arrival at Haridwar by 11:25 hrs. On arrival meet our guide and transfer to Rishikesh by road & check into the hotel. Evening free to explore the nearby area. Overnight stay at Hotel.

Day 03 : Rishikesh - Guptkashi the morning after breakfast drive towards Guptkashi via Devprayag & Rudraprayag. On the way see the confluence of River Alaknanda & Bhagirathi; from here the holy river Ganges start and flown in the plains. On arrival at Guptkashi check in to hotel. Dinner and overnight at Guptkashi.

Day 04 : Guptkashi - Gaurikund - Kedarnath Early morning after breakfast drive to Gaurikund and from here trek to Kedarnath with packed lunch. Kedarnath is a shrine of lord Shiva. In the evening visit the temple for Aarti ceremony. Dinner and overnight at Kedarnath.

Day 05 : Kedarnath - Vasukital - Kedarnath In the morning visit the temple and later after breakfast stat your trek towards Vasuki Tal. We undertake an 8 km trek to Vasukital with packed lunch. After exploring the area trek back to Kedarnath. Dinner and overnight at Kedarnath.

Day 06 : Kedarnath - Gaurikund - Rudraprayag 15 kms trek to Gaurikund with packed lunch and from here drive to Rudraprayag. Dinner and overnight at hotel.

Day 07 : Rudraprayag - Haridwar - Delhi After breakfast drive to Haridwar. On arrival at Haridwar transfer to railway station and from here catch AC Train for Delhi at 18:05 hrs. Arrival at Delhi by 22:45 hrs. on arrival meet our executive and transfer to hotel.

Day 08 : Delhi - Departure Morning sightseeing tour of the Delhi in the evening transfer to the Airport time to catch flight for onward destination.

Tour & Service End.
