Bagwal – Devidhura Fair

Description

  • The two groups (khams) of people throw stones at each other while they try to protect themselves by using big roof like shields.
  • This fair is held in the compound of the Varahi Devi temple at Devidhura on the day of Raksha Bandhan.
  • Devidhura is situated at a trijunction of Almora, Pithoragarh and Nainital districts.
  • The fair is well known for its enchanting folk songs and dances as also for its Bagwal.
  • Even watching the Bagwal is a truly thrilling experience.
  • The Devidhura fair has maintained its old vigour.
